Ronias - The Unknown World

Hoang Bao Ngan Tran

Artist statement: "'Ronias - The Unknown World' is a virtual exhibition of a fantasy world concept design. The exhibition will provide an overview of Ronias' basic structure, including social systems, geography, and ecosystems. I want to generate the experience of walking through a museum chamber where one's social and global structure is visualized through drawings and 3D models. I considered the concepts of "What if we're not the only species with high intelligence?" and "Uniqueness is singularity but also extraordinary" when I first started working on Ronias. I wanted to investigate deeply into the idea that every person is unique in some way. Being unique might be viewed negatively in some cultures, but there are times when it makes you stronger than everyone else."


Biography

Hoang Bao Ngan Tran (or Ngan Tran, also goes by Azure Fuzz on all social media), is a digital media artist focusing on illustration and concept art. She was born and raised in Vietnam before coming to the US to pursue an education in Digital Art in 2017. Ngan attended Evergreen Valley College from 2017 to 2020 and received an AA in 2-D and Studio Art. In 2020, she transferred to San Jose State University in the midst of the pandemic as an undergraduate Digital Media Art student.

Ngan sees the world as a story that only she can see and tell through her works. Her abilities in various digital mediums give her a certain value of freedom in creating her own work. She mostly expresses her contradictory worldview through various digital illustrations and character design. Her favorite drawing applications are Procreate and Clip Studio Paint.
