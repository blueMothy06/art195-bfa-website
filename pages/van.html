Dimensions

Van Nguyen

Artist statement: "Dimensions" is an immersive video projection mapping project that transports viewers into a mesmerizing 3D installation of portals unveiling multi dimensions. This transformative experience invites you to explore a world where the boundaries of reality dissolve. Through a symphony of light, sound, and technology, the exhibition inspires viewers to imagine and discover not only the showcased physical installation but also the enduring promise of themselves in alternate dimensions. It is a reflective journey into the possibilities and limitless, where the multiverse of possibilities unfolds.


Biography

Van Nguyen is a Digital Media Artist specializing in Motion Graphics, Video Arts, and Visual Design. She is intrigued by the visual aesthetics of abstraction and minimalism, and interested in bringing her artwork to life using technologies such as projection mapping on physical 3D installation. She also appreciates the beauty of expression in music and visual, and is strongly inspired by the art of multidisciplinary collaboration among artists and designers of different fields.
