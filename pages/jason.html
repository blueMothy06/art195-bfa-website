The House Tour with Tatsuo

Jason Nguyen

Artist statement: "The House Tour with Tatsuo is a virtual tour allowing the viewers to visit Tatsuo in his lovely home. Every room holds significant meaning to him so be sure to explore thoroughly and enjoy yourself. Tatsuo is a dragon child I created earlier in my life and has been with me ever since. He has featured in my other works from various 2D platformers to sketches in my sketchbook. With this house tour I hope to give Tatsuo an opportunity to meet those around me and the viewer a chance to meet Tatsuo in 3D space. Be nice to him since he does not have too many opportunities to make friends!"


Biography

Jason Nguyen is an emerging artist and soon to be graduating from San Jose State University with a degree in Digital Media Art. Through his explorations in programs from After Effects to Maya, his work explores the way artwork triggers different emotions and stories from his viewers. His passion for art comes from his love for video games and world building which he also tries to incorporate into his dance performances when he is not working in virtual space. His latest and upcoming show includes his contribution to the SJSU group BFA exhibition, “CTRL-ART-DELETE”.
