Student’s Adventure in Wonderland

Chienyu Chen

Artist statement: "Welcome to 'Student’s Adventure in Wonderland,' a virtual environment crafted to symbolize the educational journey, inspired by Alice's adventures in Wonderland. This imaginative realm comprises forests and familiar classroom objects. Every visitor to this wonderland must gather essential items to progress. Successfully completing the adventure ensures the opportunity to exit. Wishing you a delightful journey!


Biography

The virtual environment created by Chienyu Chen, “Student’s Adventure in Wonderland,” abstractly depicts the educational journey of individuals from elementary school to university graduation. This is the last artwork Chienyu Chen created as a student. She hopes to commemorate her time as a student through this virtual environment. At the same time, the piece also carries Chen’s graduation blessings for all graduating students and those who are graduating in the future.
